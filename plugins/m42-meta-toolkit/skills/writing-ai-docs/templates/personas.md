## How to Use These Personas

These personas represent our primary user types. Use them to:

- **Guide feature decisions** - Will this serve one of our personas?
- **Prioritize development** - Which persona needs this most urgently?
- **Write documentation** - Match tone and depth to persona needs
- **Design user flows** - Consider persona's technical level and goals

3. What's the impact on persona goals?
4. Does this align with persona preferences?
